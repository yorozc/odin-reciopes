John Wayne Casserole

This casserole could get done in just about an hour, 15 mins of prep time with 45 min of cooking time! You will need some biscuits, beef, taco seasonin, vegetables, sour cream, and some cheese.

Ingredients

  • biscuits
  • Beef
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Vegetables
  • Sour Cream
  • Cheese
