Fried Chicken Recipe

Create some simple fried chicken. It would have to marinate for 12 hours and then prep time is about 20 mins then cook time is about 20 min.

Ingredients

  • 4 teaspoons fine salt
  • 2 teaspoons freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 (4 pund) whole chicken
  • 2 quarts vegetable oil for frying
